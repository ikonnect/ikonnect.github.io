About


iKonnect is a student-run healthcare networking event and charity fundraiser at the University of British Columbia. This networking event is in it's ninth year running and is tailored towards undergraduate students from faculties including Sciences, Kinesiology, and Land and Food Systems. The event will allow students to gain invaluable insight and advice from experienced professionals for career paths in healthcare they are interested in.


All donations raised through student attendance and the raffle draw have been donated to local charities. In the past iKonnect has supported HOPE International as well as the local initiative The Reading Bear. This year's contributions will be going towards Covenant House. Covenant House Vancouver is an initiative that provides crisis programs, shelters and street outreach programs for over 1500 street-involved youth each year. They are a local charity dedicated to changing the lives of others by helping them get through the challenges they face.
