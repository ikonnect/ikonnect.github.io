What is iKonnect?

iKonnect is a healthcare networking event and charity fundraiser at the University of British Columbia. This networking event is tailored towards undergraduate students from faculties including Sciences, Kinesiology, and Land and Food Systems. The event will allow students to gain insight and advice from experienced professionals for career paths in healthcare they are interested in.
